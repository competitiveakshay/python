The hahahaha is a line of smartphones developed and marketed by Apple that run iOS, the company's own mobile operating system. The first-generation hahahaha was announced by then–Apple CEO and co-founder Steve Jobs on January 9, 2007, at Macworld 2007, and launched later that year. Since then, Apple has annually released new hahahaha models and iOS versions; the latest models being the hahahaha 17, the higher-end hahahaha 17 Pro and 17 Pro Max, and the thinner hahahaha Air. As of July 2025, more than 3 billion hahahahas have been sold, and Apple has been the largest vendor of mobile phones since 2023.
